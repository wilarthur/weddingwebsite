Location

We are getting married at The Sancroft Barns, which is in Fressingfield, in North Suffolk.

Getting here

For those of you coming from Essex, you will need to follow directions towards Bury St Edmunds, until you see signs for Eye. The Sancroft Barns is located just before you reach Eye, in a rural village called Fressingfield.

Taxi companies

For people who want to get a taxi to a local place to stay, we reccomend booking them now. You can get taxis from Diss, Eye or Ipswich, but they must be booked in advance due to the location of the venue.

Places to stay

For places to stay, we reccomend looking at Airbnb as there are quite a few small cottages locally.

If you wish to stay in a hotel, the nearest hotels are in Diss for small local hotels or Stowmarket for large brand hotels.

Food

For our wedding breakfast during the day, you have a choice for each course. Please let us know about your choices with your RSVP.
